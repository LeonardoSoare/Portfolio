About Us
Our mission
Heuristics developed by the Solely Company is a productivity tool, based on an algorithm which chooses the shortest path to reach a certain destination.
Based on theory of cybernetics, heuristics is an engine that finds for you the shortest paths to achieve your goals. It can take a lot of criteria and data into account, and helps you making your life a little bit simpler. It has the capabilities to learn from your feedback and from your required time for completing tasks.
Features
Accesible from any device
Heuristics is available among all platforms and devices, so it would be easier for you to access and log in your progress. Thanks to the built-in engine it doesn't require an internet connection, and whenever is accesible it would store your data so you can access it from other devices.
Customize your data
Start your journey with a plan. Set up stages for your progress, implement conditions and put costs for each activity you set up to do, so our prediction can be as accurate as possible. Also in our app our users can filter, track more than one goal and modify all of their data. In our app every user will have a to-do list, a task system and a journal included to help our users productivity.
Keep going with our notifications
Meet Stics, he is your AI assistant. He will help you stick to your daily tasks and journey. He will monitor your activity and sugest you to change some things if it will help your reach your goals faster. He is a fun robot to talk to and you can chat with him whenever you feel demotivated, he will notify you when you are not as productive as you wish to be and he will give helpful tips to increase your productivity. We hope you will all get along !
